Overview

The Faculty of Health Sciences at Trans Eurasia University prepares students for careers in healthcare, medicine, and research. Our programs are designed to meet international standards and train professionals ready to improve lives worldwide.

Research

Our faculty leads cutting-edge research in medicine, pharmacology, epidemiology, and clinical sciences. Students are encouraged to participate in projects that impact healthcare policy and innovation.

We partner with medical universities, hospitals, and research institutions worldwide, enabling international exchanges, joint research, and clinical training opportunities.
