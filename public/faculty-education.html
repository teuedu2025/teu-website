Overview

The Faculty of Education at Trans Eurasia University is committed to preparing future educators and leaders who are equipped with modern teaching skills, innovative approaches, and the ability to shape learning environments for the 21st century.

Academic Departments

  • Department of Teacher Training
  • Department of Curriculum & Instruction
  • Department of Educational Leadership & Policy
  • Department of Special & Inclusive Education

Degree Programs

Undergraduate Programs

  • Bachelor of Education (Primary Education)
  • Bachelor of Education (Secondary Education)
  • Bachelor of Educational Psychology

Postgraduate Programs

  • Master of Education (M.Ed.)
  • Master in Curriculum Studies
  • Master in Educational Leadership
  • Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Education

Research Opportunities

Research areas include teacher development, inclusive education, digital learning, curriculum innovation, and leadership in educational policy. Students collaborate with schools, ministries, and international institutions.

Facilities

  • Modern Classrooms with Smart Boards
  • Educational Research Centers
  • Teacher Training Laboratories
  • Digital Learning & E-Library

Global Engagement

We maintain partnerships with educational institutions worldwide, providing exchange opportunities, international teaching internships, and joint research projects.
